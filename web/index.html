Scenarios

Part 1 — Baseline
Raw UDP stream with zero packet loss and zero added latency. Verify the stream renders correctly end-to-end.

Run the sender locally: 
cargo run
Part 2 — 5% Packet Loss
5% of UDP datagrams are dropped at the router. Observe framing corruption and stream recovery behaviour.
Part 3 — 10% Loss + 200ms Latency
10% packet loss with 200ms of added latency. Observe the compounding effects of loss and delay on the stream.
